How it works

Make your designs real!

Document every step of the process and get your build funded + win cool prizes!

1

Design

(get approval)

Lock your idea, and start on the CAD, schematics, and firmware. Once those are, you can submit to review and get approved to start ordering parts.

2

Order parts & build

Source what you need, then solder, print, and assemble. Journal your build process for the comic page later!

3

Publish your build

Gridlock comic book

Ship photos, a short write-up, and a comic strip panel for the GRIDLOCK comic book! Then, ship it to the world!.
